The Studio Philosophy

Classical • Contemporary

Advanced RCM Specialist

Based in Bedford, Nova Scotia, my studio offers a structured yet supportive environment for students of all levels. As a Registered Music Teacher (RMT) with 30 years of experience, I emphasize a balanced approach: firm expectations for practice and progress, paired with a fair and encouraging teaching style.

Whether preparing for **RCM examinations** or learning for personal enrichment, each lesson is uniquely tailored to the student. Being named a **2023 Teacher of Distinction** reflects my commitment to developing both technical mastery and a genuine love for the instrument.
