Studio Policy Details

  • Parental involvement in the progress of students is encouraged. Students should devote a minimum of 15 mins/day to practice; theory must be completed before the lesson.
  • Lessons must be paid at the beginning of each month by Interac e-Transfer.
  • No make-ups for missed lessons. Credit applied to the following month for lessons missed by the teacher.
  • If ill, students should not attend in person. Credit will be added to fees for lessons missed due to illness.
  • Time lost due to student lateness cannot be made up.
  • Students must not enter exams or performances without teacher approval.
  • The teacher will provide a safe, supportive, and positive learning environment.
