The Studio Philosophy

Based in Bedford, Nova Scotia, my studio offers a structured yet supportive environment for students of all levels. Whether preparing for RCM examinations or learning for personal enrichment, each lesson is designed to meet the student's unique needs.

As a Registered Music Teacher (RMT), I emphasize a balanced approach: firm expectations for practice and progress, paired with a fair and encouraging teaching style.

Studio Policy

Practice

Daily practice (minimum 15-30 mins) is essential for progress. Theory assignments should be completed before each lesson.

Payments

Tuition is payable at the first lesson of each month via Interac e-Transfer to jennifer@mossmusic.ca.

Attendance

No make-ups for missed lessons. If a lesson is cancelled due to illness, a credit is applied to the following month.
