The Studio Philosophy

Based in Bedford, Nova Scotia, my studio offers a structured yet supportive environment for students of all levels. Whether preparing for RCM examinations or learning for personal enrichment, each lesson is designed to meet the student's unique needs.

As a Registered Music Teacher (RMT) with 30 years of experience, I emphasize a balanced approach: firm expectations for practice and progress, paired with a fair and encouraging teaching style.

• BSc (Dalhousie)
• AVCM (Conservatory)
• ARCT (RCM)
• NSRMTA Member

Being named a 2023 Teacher of Distinction by the Royal Conservatory of Music is a testament to the dedication I bring to every lesson, ensuring that the next generation of pianists develops both technical mastery and a lifelong love for music.
